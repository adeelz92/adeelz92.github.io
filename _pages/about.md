I am a PhD candidate at Data Visualization and Modeling (DaVim) Lab at Computer Science department of University of Houston. I am advised by [Dr. Guoning Chen](https://www2.cs.uh.edu/~chengu/) and co-advised by [Dr. Di Yang](https://yang.me.uh.edu/) I received my B.Eng. degree in Software Engineering from National University of Sciences and Technology (NUST), Islamabad, Pakistan, in 2015, my M.S. degree in Software Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China, in 2019. My research work involves topological data analysis (TDA) and scientific visualization applied to large scale turbulent fluid flows. I am passionate about extracting meaningful data and insights from large scale physical simulations using data visualization, computer graphics and topology based methods. I am proficient in computer programming (Python, C++, etc.) in support of data visualization and analysis for scientific or engineering applications.
